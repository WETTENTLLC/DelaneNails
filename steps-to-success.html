Steps to Success

DNNC Steps to Success

DNNC Steps to Success is born from Delane's struggles as a single parent. This program assists women and single mothers by introducing them to a career as a manicurist where they can schedule their time around family needs. It uplifts mothers to create a successful trajectory for themselves and their children. Delane finds great joy in providing a woman or single mother with a car each year.

DNNC Steps to Success supports women in all facets of life. We offer career guidance, personal development, and the tools needed to achieve financial independence. The program is designed to empower women and create a brighter future for themselves and their families.
