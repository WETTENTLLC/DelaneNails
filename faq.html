Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

We offer a wide range of services, including manicures, pedicures, private parties, and mobile services. Our services are designed to provide a luxurious and relaxing experience.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require a 24-hour notice for cancellations. If you do not inform us within this timeframe, you will be charged for the missed appointment.

Can I bring my children to the salon?

While we do not allow children unless they are receiving a service, we do allow mothers to bring their new babies and breastfed children. Our private rooms and toxic-free environment make for a perfect place for new moms with their precious new bundles of joy.

How can I book a mobile appointment or a private party?

Please call our salon for availability and to book a mobile appointment or a private party. Our private parties are great for birthdays, bridal showers, girls spa parties, and more!
