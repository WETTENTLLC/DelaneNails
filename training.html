Specialized Manicure Training

Important Industry Update: In 2022, the California Board of Barbering and Cosmetology eliminated practical demonstrations from licensing requirements, relying solely on written tests. This means cosmetologists may now work without demonstrating practical skills.

DNNC in partnership with DNNC Steps to Success is doubling down to provide much-needed practical training both in-person and via Zoom. Our mission is to ensure the safety and protection of the public while supporting the sustainability of careers in our industry.
