Featuring Myeshia Jefferson

Myeshia Jefferson became the co-owner of a 5-Star nail salon and assistant director of a community-based program through her hard work and participation in the DNNC Steps to Success program. With over 10 years of experience, Myeshia has dedicated her career to teaching young women how to become sustainable business owners in the beauty industry.

More About Myeshia Jefferson

Myeshia graduated from Cal State East Bay, Hayward, CA in 2010 with a BA in Criminal Justice and later earned a certification in Business and Wellness from NYU's Institute for Integrative Nutrition in 2012. She is the founder of Beauty Means Business LLC, a consulting company that guides aspiring beauty professionals. Myeshia also holds a medical manicure certification and partners with podiatrists to ensure the health and wellness of her clients.
