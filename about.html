About Delane’s Natural Nail Care

Our Story

At Delane’s Natural Nail Care, we pride ourselves on delivering exceptional nail care services with a commitment to health, wellness, and community. Founded by Delane Sims, a visionary and passionate advocate for natural beauty, our salon has grown into a haven for those seeking quality and comfort.

Meet Delane Sims

Delane Sims, the heart and soul of DNNC, has dedicated her career to transforming lives through beauty. With her extensive knowledge, unwavering dedication, and an eye for detail, Delane has built a legacy of excellence. Her journey from overcoming personal challenges to becoming a beacon of hope for many is truly inspiring.

Meet Myeshia Jefferson

Myeshia Jefferson, co-owner and a testament to the success of the DNNC Steps to Success program, is a talented and dedicated professional. Her journey from a program participant to a co-owner exemplifies the transformative power of empowerment and support. Myeshia’s passion for nail care and her commitment to uplifting others make her an invaluable asset to DNNC.

Our Accomplishments

Over the years, DNNC has garnered numerous accolades and recognition for its excellence in nail care and community service. Our unwavering commitment to quality and our clients has earned us the esteemed "Business of the Year" award by the African American Business Council, presented by the San Leandro Chamber of Commerce.

Our Vision

We believe in not just offering beauty services but also empowering individuals to lead better lives. Through our DNNC Steps to Success program, we help low-income women achieve their dreams of becoming licensed manicurists, breaking the cycle of poverty, and building a brighter future for their families.

Join us at Delane’s Natural Nail Care and be a part of our journey. We look forward to serving you and making your visit an unforgettable experience.
