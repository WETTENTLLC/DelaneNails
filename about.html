About Delane's Natural Nail Care

Delane's Natural Nails specializes in taking care of your natural nails, hands, and feet. Your experience in nail care should be relaxing, safe, and nurturing in a clean environment. That is what you will receive at Delane's Natural Nail Care—a sanctuary of love, care, and community.

Our Mission & Inspiration

We borrow a page from the great philanthropist and cosmetologist, Annie Turnbo Malone, who predated and taught Madame CJ Walker as the first Black female millionaire in the US. She was committed to making a difference in the lives of women who would ordinarily be left out of a system that was designed to disenfranchise women and minorities.

Like Annie Malone, we are here to lead women in a holistic manner to a sustainable career for themselves and their families. In the spirit of her legacy, DNNC in partnership with DNNC Steps to Success is providing essential training to empower new professionals in our industry.
