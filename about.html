About Us

Delane's Natural Nail Care (DNNC) has been a cornerstone of exceptional nail care for over 25 years. What began as a simple endeavor to earn extra money without being confined to a typical job evolved into a passionate mission inspired by my grandfather. Observing his struggles with foot and hand care as he aged, I recognized how vital mobility is to overall well-being. This realization propelled me to become a licensed foot worker, specializing in both medical nail care and the artistry of nail styling.

My grandfather, who lived to the remarkable age of 118 and was arguably the oldest man in the United States, profoundly influenced my journey. Before his passing, he not only inspired my nail business but also sparked the creation of DNNC's Steps to Success initiative. Our mission at DNNC is to provide luxurious spa treatments at affordable prices, ensuring that quality service is accessible to everyone. Whether it's a private room experience, a celebratory bottle of champagne, or a lively nail party, we deliver exceptional service without the hefty price tag.
