Our Services

Manicure

Enjoy a relaxing manicure tailored to your nail care needs. We use the finest products to ensure the health and beauty of your nails. This service includes nail shaping, cuticle care, and a nourishing hand massage.

Pedicure

Indulge in a luxurious pedicure experience designed to rejuvenate your feet and leave them feeling refreshed. Includes foot soak, nail trimming, and an extended foot massage.

Specialized Nail Care

Our specialized nail care services cater to seniors and individuals needing prosthetic precision care. This includes medical-grade pedicures and advanced treatments for nail restoration.

Custom Nail Designs

Express your creativity with our custom nail designs, tailored to your unique preferences and style. Choose from a wide array of colors, patterns, and art techniques to achieve your perfect look.

Medi Spa Services

Medical Pedicure

Designed for clients with medical concerns, our Medi Spa pedicure offers specialized foot care to improve overall foot health.

Advanced Nail Restoration

Using state-of-the-art technology, we offer advanced nail restoration services for damaged or weakened nails.
