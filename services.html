Our Services

Comprehensive Services for Hands & Feet

Spa Manicure

Experience a luxurious manicure that includes nail shaping, cuticle care, exfoliation, a relaxing hand massage, and your choice of polish. Our focus is on the health of your nails and hands.

Spa Pedicure

Indulge in our spa pedicure featuring a soothing foot soak, nail shaping, cuticle care, exfoliation, a relaxing foot massage, and polish application. We prioritize the health and care of your feet.

Manicure & Pedicure

Enjoy both manicure and pedicure services together, ensuring complete care for your hands and feet.

Specialized Foot Treatments

Our specialized foot treatments are designed to address specific concerns such as calluses, cracked heels, and foot pain. These treatments include exfoliation, hydration, and targeted care to ensure your feet are healthy and beautiful.

Paraffin Aroma Vapors Treatment

Enjoy the ultimate relaxation with our paraffin aroma vapors treatment. This service includes a soothing paraffin wax dip, infused with essential oils to hydrate and soften your skin.

Deep Moisturizing Hand & Feet Treatment

This treatment provides intense hydration for your hands and feet, leaving your skin feeling soft and rejuvenated.

Prosthetic Toenail Remedies

We offer prosthetic remedies for missing toenails. Our expert technicians will provide custom solutions that look natural and feel comfortable.

Candy's Touch

A signature service that combines nail care with a personalized touch, ensuring a unique and delightful experience.

Private Parties

Host a private nail party with your friends or family. Enjoy our luxurious nail services in a private setting, complete with complimentary champagne and personalized attention.
